About

Technology should make work better, not heavier.

I architect and lead enterprise SaaS implementations with a simple intention: make complex systems feel clear and empowering. Across startups and large organizations, I’ve built and delivered high-impact digital workflows that improve how teams operate day to day.

I care about the human side of software—alignment, adoption, and the lived experience of the people inside the system. When architecture is thoughtful and execution is steady, work becomes lighter and outcomes stronger.

Goodgrow is my life's work — a way to tell the stories about the natural goods we consume everyday. We're building a resilient, ethical marketplace of curated, high-quality natural goods. When I’m not behind a screen, I bike, sail, and grow plants.
